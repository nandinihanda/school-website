was established in 2002

Multipurpose Hall Auditorium. Assemblies, exhibitions, activities, indoor sport etc are conducted in two large M.P. Hall/ Auditoriums.

was established in 2002

A social science lab is in use to facilitate the enhancement of cultural and aesthetic values among students. The lab besides holding maps, charts and models has a well furnishes conference hall and facilities.
